# Hero's Journey (Monomyth)

## Overview

Joseph Campbell's universal story structure found across cultures. Character goes on transformative journey.

---

## 12 Stages (Simplified for Ads)

### 1. Ordinary World
Character in normal life before adventure

### 2. Call to Adventure
Problem or opportunity presents itself

### 3. Refusal of Call
Initial hesitation or doubt

### 4. Meeting the Mentor
Guidance or product introduction

### 5. Crossing Threshold
Commits to change/using product

### 6. Tests, Allies, Enemies
Faces challenges, learns product

### 7. Approach
Prepares for major challenge

### 8. Ordeal
Biggest test/problem

### 9. Reward
Success with product

### 10. Road Back
Returns to normal life transformed

### 11. Resurrection
Final test or demonstration

### 12. Return with Elixir
Shares success, transformed

---

## Condensed for Short-Form (30s)

```yaml
Ordinary_World (3s): Character with problem
Call_to_Adventure (2s): Discovers product
Crossing_Threshold (5s): Tries product
Tests (8s): Uses product, overcomes challenges
Ordeal (4s): Biggest benefit demonstrated
Reward (5s): Success, transformation
Return (3s): Happy ending, CTA
```

---

## Example: Fitness Product

```yaml
S1 (3s): Person tired, out of shape (Ordinary World)
S2 (2s): Sees fitness product ad (Call)
S3 (5s): Decides to try it (Threshold)
S4 (8s): Training montage with product (Tests)
S5 (4s): Completes challenging workout (Ordeal)
S6 (5s): Transformed, confident (Reward)
S7 (3s): Inspiring others, logo (Return)
```
